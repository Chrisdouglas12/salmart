Frequently Asked Questions – Salmart by Salmart Technologies

Insights from Christian Friday Douglas (Chris Douglas), Founder of Salmart Technologies
Updated: August 5, 2025

Welcome to the Salmart FAQ hub. Whether you're new here or you've been Browse for a while, this page is your go-to for honest answers to common questions about how things work on our platform.

We're still growing, and while not all features are live yet, we’re building every part of this with real needs in mind — yours.

🔐 Accounts & Security

Q: Is it safe to use Salmart?

Yes. Salmart is built with secure authentication, verified payments, and protected messaging. We don’t tolerate scams, and we’re putting extra steps in place to protect both buyers and sellers.

Q: Can I use Salmart without creating an account?

You can browse freely, but to buy, sell, or chat, you'll need an account. Creating one is simple and free.

💳 Payments

Q: How do payments work?

Buyers pay directly on Salmart through our secure Paystack integration. Funds are held safely until the buyer confirms delivery.

Q: Does Salmart take a commission?

Yes — a small commission is taken from the seller’s payout after the buyer confirms delivery. This supports platform maintenance and security.

📦 Delivery & Deals

Q: Does Salmart deliver products?

Not directly. Sellers and buyers arrange delivery themselves, but Salmart protects payments until delivery is confirmed.

Q: How do I confirm delivery?

On your Deals page, click Confirm Delivery once you’ve received the product in good condition. This releases funds to the seller.

🛠 Platform Features

Q: What is Bargain Mode?

It’s a feature under development that allows buyers to make price offers automatically — no back-and-forth chats needed.

Q: Can I edit my post after uploading it?

Yes, you can. We provide an Edit Post button that redirects you to the original post form.

Q: Can I message sellers directly?

Yes! You can chat with sellers using our real-time chat feature — just like WhatsApp, but with built-in safety.
