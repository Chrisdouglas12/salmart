The Vision Behind Salmart

In a country where online scams and delivery disappointments have discouraged many from transacting online, Salmart was built with a clear mission:

To protect buyers and empower sellers in the online marketplace.

Salmart aims to restore trust in digital commerce by using advanced technology to bridge the gap between honest buyers and verified sellers.

How It Works

Salmart allows users to:

  1. Create accounts as buyers or sellers
  2. List and discover products from different states across Nigeria.
  3. Chat directly with sellers or buyers before and after placing an order.
  4. Pay through a secure escrow system, where funds are held by Salmart until delivery is confirmed.
  5. Release payments to the seller only when the buyer confirms that the product has been received and is as described.

This protects buyers from fraud and ensures sellers get paid fairly.

What Makes Salmart Unique?

  • Social-style messaging: Chat in real-time with sellers and buyers.
  • Scam protection: Sellers are verified and transactions are monitored.
  • PWA technology: Works offline and loads fast even with low data or slow network.
  • Smart delivery & tracking: Know the status of your orders with real-time updates.
