About Me

Hi, I'm Christian Friday Douglas, popularly known as Chris Douglas. I'm a 23-year-old Nigerian software developer and entrepreneur, and the founder of Salmart Technologies.

My journey in tech started out of a passion to solve real problems around me. Over the years, I have dedicated my time to learning, building, and experimenting with digital solutions that bring value to people.

In 2024, I began building Salmart, a social e-commerce marketplace designed to make online buying and selling in Nigeria safer, more transparent, and more rewarding for both buyers and sellers.

I believe technology should restore trust in our society, and that's the mission driving my work at Salmart Technologies.

Featured Project

Salmart – Social E-commerce Marketplace

www.salmartonline.com.ng

A platform where Nigerians can buy and sell safely, addressing the critical trust gap in online commerce.

💳 Escrow Payment System (powered by Paystack)
💬 Real-time Chat between buyers and sellers
🔔 Live Notifications for transactions, messages, likes, and comments
👥 Social Features like following users, product engagement, and chat bargaining
💰 Automated Payouts to sellers after delivery confirmation
Impact: Salmart solves the fear of fraud in online commerce by protecting both buyers and sellers through innovative escrow technology and social verification.
