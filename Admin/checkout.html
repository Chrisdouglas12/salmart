Buyer Safety Rules

  1. Before making payment, confirm with the seller that the product is still available.
  2. Never pay directly into a seller's personal bank account. Payments must go through Salmart's escrow system for your protection.
  3. Do not pay for delivery or waybill to the seller. Arrange pay-on-delivery with the logistics provider/Driver.
  4. Click the “Confirm Delivery” button only after you have received and verified your order.
  5. If the seller does not deliver or becomes unresponsive after payment, you can request a refund here.
  6. Always communicate through Salmart chat to keep a record of your conversation in case of disputes.
  7. Inspect your product immediately upon delivery before confirming receipt.

All Payments are processed by Paystack
