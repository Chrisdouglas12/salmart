Community Standards




Welcome to Salmart! To ensure a safe, trustworthy, and enjoyable experience for all users, we have established the following community guidelines. By using this platform, you agree to follow these rules.

1. Respect and Integrity

Treat all users with respect, regardless of differences in opinion, background, or beliefs. Hate speech, harassment, bullying, or any form of discrimination is strictly prohibited. Do not engage in fraudulent activities, including scams, fake reviews, or misleading advertisements.

2. Buying and Selling Responsibly

Sellers must provide accurate descriptions, images, and prices for their products. Buyers should communicate clearly and follow through with purchases. All transactions should be conducted within the platform to ensure security and accountability. Any attempt to bypass platform fees or escrow services is not allowed.

3. Prohibited Content and Products

Do not post or sell illegal, counterfeit, or prohibited items, including but not limited to: Weapons, drugs, or harmful substances Stolen goods Fraudulent financial services Explicit or adult content Listings that contain misleading or false claims will be removed.

4. Secure and Fair Transactions

We use an escrow payment system to protect both buyers and sellers. Buyers should confirm receipt of an item before funds are released to the seller. Chargebacks, unauthorized refunds, or fraudulent disputes will result in account suspension.

5. Privacy and Safety

Do not share personal information (phone numbers, addresses, banking details) in public forums or comments. Avoid engaging in transactions outside the platform, as we cannot guarantee your safety. If you suspect fraud or misconduct, report it immediately to our support team.

6. Spam and Misuse

Do not post repetitive, irrelevant, or misleading content. Fake accounts, multiple accounts for deceptive purposes, or bots are not allowed. Self-promotion should be relevant to the platform’s purpose and comply with advertising rules.

7. Enforcement and Reporting

Violations of these guidelines may result in warnings, content removal, suspension, or permanent bans. Users can report violations, and our team will take appropriate action. We reserve the right to update these guidelines at any time.

By participating in Salmart, you help build a trusted and thriving community. Let’s work together to create a safe and fair marketplace!
