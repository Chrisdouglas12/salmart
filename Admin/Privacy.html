We collect different types of information to provide and improve our services, including:

Personal Information

When you create an account or use our services, we may collect:

  • Full name

  • Phone number
  • Home or business address
  • Profile picture
  • Bank account details

Transaction and Financial Data

  • Purchase history
  • Payment confirmations
  • Escrow and settlement details
  • Refund requests
  • Paystack transaction references

Usage Data

  • IP address

We use your information to:

  • Detect fraud and enhance security

We may share your information with:

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

We implement strong technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. These include:

  • Regular security audits
